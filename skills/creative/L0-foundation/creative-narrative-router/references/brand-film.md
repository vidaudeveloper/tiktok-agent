# brand_film — Brand film

> ID: `brand_film`  
> Use when: brand values, tone, manifesto — **weak SKU list, weak hard CTA**.

## When to use

- “Brand film”, “brand story”, “who we are”, “manifesto”, “not a product demo”
- Product as symbol inside a brand world, not feature demo
- 30–90s

## Beat map — 30s

| Beat | Duration | Visual |
|------|----------|--------|
| `brand_world` | 0–8s | Palette, texture, environment |
| `values` | 8–18s | Ideas in imagery (not slogan walls) |
| `human_touch` | 18–24s | People × brand relationship |
| `signature` | 24–28s | Motif / icon |
| `logo_resolve` | 28–30s | Logo — soft |

## Scene types

`brand_motif` · `lifestyle_montage` · `texture_detail` · `logo_end`

## Forbidden

- SKU spec sheets
- Invented brand history / awards
- Aggressive “Buy now” unless user asked

## Voiceover

30s: ~35–50 words. Poetic, short lines.
